Field Guide

Idea Fields

Problem
The specific pain point this solves. What real people are complaining about in the trend data.
Solution
What you'd actually build. The product described in 1-2 sentences.
Monetization
How the idea makes money: SaaS (monthly subscription), one-time purchase, freemium (free + paid tier), affiliate (commission from referrals), or marketplace fees (cut of transactions).

Validation & Evidence

Validation Steps
2-3 quick actions to test if the problem is real before you build anything. Examples: post a poll, put up a landing page, DM potential users, search for competitors.
Evidence
Direct links to the HackerNews stories or Reddit posts that prove people actually have this problem. Click through to read the original discussions.

AI Buildability

What it means
Can this idea be built primarily with AI coding tools (Claude Code, Cursor, Codex)? Rates the viability of vibe-coding the MVP, lists required technologies (database, auth, AI APIs, etc.), and estimates calendar time and upfront launch cost.
high
Fully AI-buildable. Standard web stack, no exotic integrations. A weekend with AI tools.
medium
Mostly AI-buildable but has tricky parts — browser extensions, real-time features, or complex APIs that need manual work.
low
AI can scaffold it, but core logic needs deep domain expertise, custom infra, or hardware integration.

Complexity

low
< 1 week. Simple script, API wrapper, or content site.
medium
1-2 weeks. Full web app with auth, payments, basic features.
high
3-4 weeks. Complex integrations, SDKs, multiple moving parts.
